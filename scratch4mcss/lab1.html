Scratch for MCSS

Lab 1: Let's Start Scratching!

Welcome to Scratch for MCSS! In this Summer 2022 workshop you will learn to use one of the most engaging, educational, and just plain awesome software technologies there is. Scratch is great for telling stories, making simulations and games, and learning 21st century media skills. It is a constructivist, media rich learning environment with an online community that hosts millions of example projects that you can learn from.

Activity:

  1. Create your first Scratch project: Visiting the Four Quadrants.
