Web Development Weekly Grading Rubric

Grade Description
A Clear evidence of time on task is present through regular and timely commits. Attention to detail is evidenced by having validation links on all web pages for both HTML and CSS that give green bars validated. The design work show care and pride of authorship, and meets all technical requirements. Responsibility is demonstrated by fixing and closing any issues filed the previous week.
B Work exhibits most of the characteristics described at the A level above, but may have a few uncaught errors or provide insufficient evidence of engagement to warrent the highest evaluation.
C Work is partially complete and mostly correct, but important elements are missing or inaccurate.
D Some work was completed, but it shows only minimal effort and does not provide sufficient evidence of learning mastery.
E No evidence of work presented.
